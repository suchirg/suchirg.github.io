The Legend of Zelda (NES) Remake

This is a novel game in which player's must make it to the finish line of each level before all the lights in the level go out. This was developed independently over a course of two weeks as the 2nd project of EECS 494 at the University of Michigan.

I used C# with Unity to develop the game.
